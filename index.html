SKATEBOARDING

Skateboarding is an action sport originating in the United States that involves riding and performing tricks using a skateboard, as well as a recreational activity, an art form, an entertainment industry job, and a method of transportation.

Ollie/Pop Shuvit - Tricks

This is considered by many as the easiest trick to master. It combines pop and an ollie, so it is called the “pop-shuvit”. This might be difficult to get the first time, but after some practice, you will be able to land this trick easily!.

Nollie- skateboarding moves

The nollie is all about balance. You need to place your foot on the nose of the board while doing this trick. If you don’t have enough balance, it might be hard and even painful to land..
